About us

Your Destination for Relaxation and Radiant Skin

Welcome to our luxurious spa and skincare center, where we believe that self-care is an essential part of a balanced lifestyle. With a passion for providing exceptional experiences and promoting healthy skin, we strive to create a sanctuary for you to unwind and rejuvenate.

Our dedicated team of experienced estheticians and therapists are committed to delivering personalized treatments tailored to your unique needs. We combine the power of advanced skincare techniques, high-quality products, and a tranquil atmosphere to help you achieve a state of bliss and enhance your natural beauty.
Graduated in Pharmacy with a Master in Cosmetic Sciences at Farleigh Dickinson University in the United States. In 1992, after an experience in international companies in the cosmetics sector, he joined the Davines Group. Today he is leading the company with a strong commitment to ethics and sustainability, product innovation and international business growth. His vision of Sustainable Beauty: a combination of cutting-edge chemistry and respect for the environment and society, has led the Group to become a B Corporation.

At our spa, we offer a wide range of indulgent services, including revitalizing facials, soothing massages, therapeutic body treatments, and much more. Whether you seek relaxation, stress relief, or a complete skincare transformation, we are here to guide you on your wellness journey.
